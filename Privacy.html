1. Overview & Introduction

Welcome to PhysicsWiz! We're a team of passionate educators and engineers building the next generation of science learning. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect from you, how we use it, and what rights you have.

The short version: We collect minimal data — only what's needed to run PhysicsWiz and let you learn. We never sell your data. We never use it for ads. We don't share it with third parties unless we absolutely have to (and we'll tell you when). Your data is yours.

🎯 Our commitment: We're built for students, by students. Privacy isn't a feature we added — it's woven into our DNA from day one.
📊
2. What Data Do We Collect?

We collect data in the following categories:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address, name (optional), and password (hashed, never stored in plain text).
  • Learning Data: Your progress through chapters, quiz scores, completed topics, and time spent on each concept. This helps personalize your learning journey.
  • Interaction Data: Which features you use (animations, Ask AI, sliders), how often you interact, and for how long. We use this to improve the platform.
  • Feedback: When you submit feedback or contact support, we store that conversation to help you better.
  • Device Information: Your device type, browser, OS, and IP address (for security and analytics only).
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized data about how features are used. Never personally identifiable.
⚠️ We do NOT collect: Location data, biometric data, payment information (we're free!), social media profiles, or any "shadow data" for advertising purposes.
⚙️
3. How We Use Your Data

Your data powers PhysicsWiz. Here's exactly what we do with it:

  • Service Delivery: We use your account info and progress data to provide the learning experience you signed up for.
  • Personalization: Your quiz scores and completed topics let us recommend the next chapter and adapt content difficulty.
  • Improvements: Aggregated usage data helps us identify which animations confuse students, which features are loved, and where to build next.
  • Support: Your email and feedback let us respond when you have questions or issues.
  • Security: Device and IP data help us detect fraud and keep your account safe.
  • Legal Compliance: We keep records to comply with laws (child safety, data protection, etc.).

That's it. We never use your data for:

  • Targeted advertising or behavioral tracking
  • Selling to third-party marketers
  • Training AI models on your content
  • Profiling or discrimination
  • Anything you didn't explicitly consent to
🤝
4. Who Else Sees Your Data?

Short answer: Almost nobody.

We use a few trusted third-party services — only when necessary:

  • Hosting (Google Cloud): Your data lives on encrypted servers. Google can't see it; they just provide the infrastructure.
  • Email (SendGrid): We use their service to send you password resets and support replies. They see the email address, nothing else.
  • Analytics (Plausible Analytics): Privacy-first analytics that show us aggregated trends. No personal data is sent.
  • Support (Zendesk): Only if you contact us — then we share your message to respond faster.

We never: Sell your data. Share it with marketers. Use it for secondary purposes. Give it to data brokers. Period.

🌍 Legal requirement: If a government agency demands your data with a valid legal order, we're required by law to comply. We'll notify you first unless we're legally prohibited.
⏰
5. How Long Do We Keep Your Data?

We keep your data only as long as you're using PhysicsWiz, plus a bit longer for safety:

  • Active Account: Your learning progress is kept for as long as your account is active. It's yours to access anytime.
  • After Deletion: When you delete your account, we remove all personal data within 30 days. Aggregate analytics (which don't identify you) are kept longer.
  • Backups: We keep encrypted backups for 90 days for disaster recovery. After that, deleted data is gone for good.
  • Legal/Security: We may keep minimal records (IP addresses, timestamps) for up to 1 year for security investigations.

You can request your data anytime, and you can delete your account anytime. No questions asked.

👤
6. Your Privacy Rights

Under laws like GDPR, COPPA, and CCPA, you have these rights — and we respect them:

  • Right to Access: You can download all your data in a machine-readable format (CSV, JSON).
  • Right to Correct: You can update your name, email, or other personal info anytime in settings.
  • Right to Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data with one click.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can opt out of optional analytics and emails anytime.
  • Right to Object: You can object to processing for any reason, and we'll honor it.
  • Right to Portability: You can move your data to another service if you choose.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@physicswiz.com or visit your account settings. We'll respond within 30 days, always.

🛡️
7. How We Protect Your Data

Security isn't a checkbox — it's our default state. Here's what we do:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data travels over HTTPS/TLS 1.3. Snooping is impossible.
  • Encryption at Rest: Your data is encrypted on our servers using AES-256.
  • Passwords: We never store passwords. We store cryptographic hashes using bcrypt. Even we can't see your password.
  • Access Control: Only essential staff can access data, and only with audit logs.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct security audits every quarter and bug bounties to find vulnerabilities.
  • Two-Factor Authentication: You can enable 2FA on your account for extra security.
  • Incident Response: If we detect a breach (unlikely, but possible), we notify you within 72 hours.
🔒 Zero-Knowledge Architecture: Even in a worst-case scenario where someone hacked our servers, they'd get encrypted data they can't decrypt. Your password is the only key, and we don't have it.
👶
8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

PhysicsWiz is built for students, many of whom are under 13. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • Minimal Data Collection: From anyone under 13, we collect only what's essential: email (for account recovery), class level, and learning progress.
  • No Tracking: We don't use cookies, pixel tracking, or behavioral analytics for users under 13.
  • No Third-Party Ads: We have zero ad networks. No tracking pixels. No data sharing with marketers.
  • Parental Consent: For users under 13, we recommend parental consent. Parents can request access to their child's data anytime.
  • Age Verification: We use basic age verification (no ID required — just honesty). If you're under 13, we apply stricter protections automatically.

For Parents: You can email parents@physicswiz.com to review, correct, or delete your child's data. We respond within 48 hours.
